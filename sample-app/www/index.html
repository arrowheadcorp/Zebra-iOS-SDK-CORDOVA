To fully unpair a scanner:

  1. Go to iOS Settings
  2. Select Bluetooth
  3. Find the scanner in the paired devices list
  4. Tap the "i" icon next to it
  5. Select "Forget This Device"

Pairing Instructions:

  1. Make sure the scanner is turned on
  2. Generate the pairing barcode below
  3. Scan the barcode with your Zebra scanner
  4. The scanner will beep to confirm pairing
  5. Go to the Scanners tab to verify connection
